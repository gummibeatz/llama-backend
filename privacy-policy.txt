Psychic-Llama Privacy Policy
======

This privacy policy was last modified on June 2, 2015.

Michael Chary operates psychic-llama in partnership with Celdara Medical, LLC. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information we receive from users of this App. 

We use you Personal Information only for providing and improving the App. By using this App, you agree to the ocllection and use of information in accordance with this policy. 

__Information Collection and Use__
 
  While using our App we may monitor your pattern of interaction with different facets of the app. We may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact you or identify you. We will do this only in the context of creating an account for users to store their data for later. Users can choose not to create an account. If a user chooses not to create an account then we will not ask that user to provide any personally identifiable information. Personally identifiable information may include, but is not limited to your name, location and e-mail address. 

 __Log Data__

   As with many apps we may collect information such as the type of mobile device you are using. The information includes but is not limited to location, time spent looking at each portion of the app.
